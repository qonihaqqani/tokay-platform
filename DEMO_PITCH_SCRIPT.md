## üéØ **Demo Tips & Best Practices**

### **Technical Preparation:**
- **Internet:** Have backup mobile hotspot
- **Browser:** Chrome or Firefox, latest version
- **Cache:** Clear browser cache before demo
- **Tabs:** Only open necessary tabs

### **Presentation Skills:**
- **Pace:** Don't rush - let each feature sink in
- **Interaction:** Ask questions: "How many of you run restaurants?" "What's your biggest business challenge?"
- **Language:** Mix BM and English naturally: "Ini sangat power, you know?"
- **Energy:** Show enthusiasm - this is exciting!

### **Handling Questions:**
- **Technical:** "Our team handles all the technical setup. You just focus on your business."
- **Price:** "We have flexible plans for all business sizes. Let's talk after the demo."
- **Competition:** "Others give you data. We give you intelligence and actionable advice."

### **Backup Plans:**
- **If API is slow:** "As you can see, we're loading real-time data. In production, this is even faster."
- **If feature fails:** "Let me show you another amazing feature while that loads..."
- **If internet dies:** Have screenshots ready as backup
